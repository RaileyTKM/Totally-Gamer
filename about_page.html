Our project, ‘Totally Gamers’, simulates a video game digital distribution platform with PHP and Oracle database. In our model, the aspects of players and developers are established, where they share similar information (e.g. Nickname, ID, Password) yet differs in behavior (e.g. a developer cannot purchase games, while a player cannot upload games). A user is able to access and update personal information, insert items into the database (e.g. purchase game, create forum), and querying conditioned information (e.g. search game by its type). Furthermore, the website also provides aggregated information such as average playtime of each game, providing the users with analytical data that they can refer to.
